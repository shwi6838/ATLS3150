Vinyl Records

Vinyl Records and physical media have made a comeback. I have started collecting vinyl records after my friends introduced me. I am a huge music fan and I love the way they sound, and I got my own turntable and speakers. I recommend people check out Paradise Found Records in Boulder.

Here are some of my favorite albums that I have in my collection:

  • Mt Joy Live at Redrocks

  • Tyler Childers - Purgatory

  • A Tribe Called Quest - Midnight Marauders

  • Tash Sultana - Flow State

  • Dominic Fike - What Could Possibly Go Wrong

  • Molowda and the Humble - Molowda and the Humble

  • Khurangbin + Men I Trust Live at Echo Beach
